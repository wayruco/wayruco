## Mission Continuity

### Wayru Inc.'s Legacy
Wayru Inc. made the historic decision to transition its core technology to open source, recognizing that:
- Decentralization requires decentralized governance
- Community ownership ensures long-term sustainability
- Open source enables faster innovation and adoption
- The mission is bigger than any single company

### WayruCO's Commitment
WayruCO honors this legacy by:
- Maintaining the open source nature of all core technology
- Continuing the mission of internet access as a human right
- Building on the technical foundation created by Wayru Inc.
- Expanding the community and impact globally
